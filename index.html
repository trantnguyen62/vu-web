Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about property taxes, appeals, and our tools.

The best way is to compare your property's assessed value per square foot to similar properties in your neighborhood using our Property Tax Tool. If your neighbors with similar homes are assessed significantly lower, you likely have grounds for an appeal.

Filing an appeal directly with the Cook County Assessor's Office is completely free. You do not need to hire a lawyer to file an appeal, especially for residential properties. Our tools provide the data you need to file yourself.

Appeal windows are open for specific townships at different times of the year, usually for a period of 30 days. Check our "Appeal Deadlines" section or the official Assessor's website to see if your township is currently open for appeals.

We use publicly available data directly from the Cook County Assessor's Office and the Cook County Open Data Portal. While we strive for 100% accuracy, we always recommend verifying final numbers with official county records before filing legal documents.
